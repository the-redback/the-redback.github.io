Kiki & Meelo

The Permanent Residents!

Kiki was our first introduction to life with cats. We went to a shelter, and she chose us! She is a very sweet and loving cat, and she has been a wonderful companion. She never does anything wrong, the perfect firstborn!

Meelo had a much rougher start. Her mother was involved in an accident, and she was rescued by kind Samaritans when she was only 3–4 weeks old. Since then, she has grown into a lovable velcro baby who always wants to be close to her humans. She may be a bit crazy at times, but she makes up for it with endless affection and personality.

Kiki — Teacher's pet. Mom's favorite. Dad's favorite too.
